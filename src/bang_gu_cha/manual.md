# Bang-gu-cha (방구차) - User Manual

Welcome to **Bang-gu-cha**, an exciting chase game where you must collect flags while avoiding enemy cars!

## 1. Getting Started

### Character Selection
When you first load the game, you will see the **Character Selection Screen**.
![Character Selection](file:///Users/changjurhee/Projects/GAG/src/bang_gu_cha/manual_images/selection.png)

1.  Hover over the faces to see them highlight.
2.  **Click** on your favorite character face to start the game.

## 2. How to Play

### Objective
Your goal is to collect all the **Yellow Flags** scattered around the map.
![Gameplay](file:///Users/changjurhee/Projects/GAG/src/bang_gu_cha/manual_images/gameplay.png)

-   **Blue Car**: This is you! Your selected character face is displayed on top.
-   **Red Cars**: These are enemies. Avoid them!
-   **Yellow Dots**: These are flags. Collect them for points.

### Controls
-   **Arrow Keys**: Move Up, Down, Left, Right.
-   **Spacebar**: Use **Fart (Smoke Screen)**.
-   **Enter**: Restart the game (after Game Over or Win).

### Abilities
-   **Fart (Smoke Screen)**:
    -   Press `Spacebar` to release a smoke cloud.
    -   Costs **10 Fuel**.
    -   If an enemy touches the smoke, they will be **stunned** for 3 seconds.

## 3. Game Over & Winning

### Game Over
If an enemy car touches you, the game ends.
![Game Over](file:///Users/changjurhee/Projects/GAG/src/bang_gu_cha/manual_images/gameover.png)
-   Press **Enter** to try again with the same character.
-   Refresh the page to choose a different character.

### Winning
Collect all flags to win the stage!
